Sweet Chili Chicken Tacos

Here's a really simple recipe I came up with when I was messing around in the kitchen. My girlfriend absolutely loves it every time I make it.

It's not sophisticated in the slightest, but I think the simplicity of it really helps it in the end. Adjust according to portion size & taste.


Time to make:

Prep time: 15 min

Cook time: 20 min

Total Time: 30 min

What you'll need:

Ingredients:

  • 1 raw chicken breast
  • corn starch
  • chili flakes
  • white pepper
  • salt
  • paprika
  • garlic powder
  • Thai Kitchen Sweet Red Chili Sauce
  • tortillas
  • olive oil

You'll also need:

  • a stove-top
  • a cutting board
  • a knife
  • a bowl for the raw chicken
  • a bowl for the cooked chicken
  • a pan or wok
  • a flat pan for the tortillas

Directions

  1. Dice the chicken breast(s) into little squares.
  2. In a large bowl, coat the diced chicken in corn starch. Don't worry about going overboard. You want every piece to be coated.
  3. In a large heated wok, pour 1tbsp of olive oil, making sure to coat the entire wok.
  4. Place the coated chicken into the wok, spread evenly. Add extra olive oil.
  5. Add salt, chili flakes, white pepper, paprika, garlic powder, & any other spices
  6. For the next 10 minutes, stir & flip chicken, making sure to spread spices evenly & ensuring that every piece is cooked thoroughly.
  7. On a separate burner, heat up a flat pan to medium. Make sure the pan is fully heated. Cook tortillas, flipping once each tortilla expands, until a crust forms on both sides. Place tortillas onto their own plate for cooling.
  8. Once the chicken is fully cooked, remove from burner & place cooked chicken into a clean bowl. Pour a generous amount of Thai Kitchen Sweet Red Chili Sauce & toss, making sure every piece is coated entirely.

Enjoy!
