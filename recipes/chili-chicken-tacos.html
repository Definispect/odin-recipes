Sweet Chili Chicken Tacos

Here's a really simple recipe I came up with when I was messing around in the kitchen. My girlfriend absolutely loves it every time I make it.

It's not sophisticated in the slightest, but I think the simplicity of it really helps it in the end. Adjust according to portion size & taste.


Time to make:

Prep time: 15 min

Cook time: 20 min

Total Time: 30 min

What you'll need:

Ingredients:

  • 1 raw chicken breast
  • corn starch
  • chili flakes
  • white pepper
  • salt
  • paprika
  • garlic powder
  • Thai Kitchen sweet red chili sauce

You'll also need:

  • an oven
  • a 9x5" loaf pan
  • a small bowl
  • a small-sized mixing bowl
  • a large-sized mixing bowl
  • a grating tool for the lemon zest
  • a spatula to fold the batter
  • an electric mixer
  • a basting brush
  • (optional) a lemon juicer

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F. Grease loaf pan.
  2. In a small bowl, combine flour with baking powder, salt & lemon zest.
  3. In a large bowl, beat butter with the electric mixer. Slowly beat in sugar for 2 minutes, then beat in eggs, vanilla, & milk.
  4. Fold the dry mix into the wet mix until combined, careful not to over-mix.
  5. Pour the batter into the loaf pan, smoothing the top.
  6. Place the pan with the batter into the center of the oven, baking for 55-65 minutes, or until golden-brown.
  7. In a separate bowl, stir icing sugar into lemon juice to create a sugary glaze.
  8. Once the loaf is ready, take it out of the oven to let it cool for 10 minutes. Cover the loaf with the glaze, & enjoy!

I recommend trying the loaf first before making any modifications, but the simplicity of the recipe opens itself to a ton of variations. Play around with the recipe & make sure to have fun!
