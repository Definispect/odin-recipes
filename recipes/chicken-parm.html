Chicken Parmesan


Description:

Who doesn't love a bit of chicken parm? Simple & delicious, the combo of breaded chicken, tomato sauce, & melted parmesan cheese... it's just yum.

I don't have any special recipes to show for this time around. Honestly, I don't even have that much experience cooking chicken parm... or even eating it LOL

& THAT'S WHY I'M TAKING THE RECIPE FROM HERE!! (thanks Holly).

Time to make:

Prep time: 15 min

Bake time: 25 min

Total Time: 40 min

What you'll need:

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2/3 cup Panko bread crumbs
  • 2/3 cup Italian seasoning bread crumbs
  • 2 teaspoons fresh parsley
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 24 ounces marinara sauce
  • 1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1 teaspoon chopped basil
  • 1 teaspoon chopped parsley

All recipe substitutions can be found on the original blog post.

You'll also need:

  • an oven
  • a 9×13 baking dish
  • a frying pan/skillet
  • a meat thermometer
  • 2 shallow bowls
  • a meat mallet
  • paper towels
  • mixing & measuring tools

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F.
  2. Place the flour in a shallow bowl or dish. Place the eggs in a second shallow dish & beat with a fork.
  3. In a third shallow dish, combine Panko, Italian seasoned crumbs, grated Parmesan, 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, ½ teaspoon salt, & ¼ teaspoon pepper.
  4. Using a meat mallet, pound the chicken breasts to ½-inch thickness. After pounding, if they're very large, you can cut them in half. Pat the chicken dry with paper towels & season with ½ teaspoon salt & ¼ teaspoon black pepper.
  5. Dip chicken into the flour & shake to remove any excess. Dip chicken in beaten eggs, then into the bread crumb mixture & gently press to adhere.
  6. Preheat the oil over medium-high heat in a large skillet. Brown the chicken for about 2 minutes per side or until golden. It does not need to cook through.
  7. In the bottom of a 9×13 baking dish, add 1 ½ cups of marinara sauce. Add the browned chicken. Top each piece of chicken with 2 tablespoons of marinara sauce in the center. Top with mozzarella & shredded parmesan.
  8. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until golden & bubbly & the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165°F with an instant-read thermometer.
  9. Sprinkle with fresh basil & parsley & serve over pasta.
