Lemon Loaf Recipe

This is the classic version of the Lemon Loaf recipe that once existed on Chatelaine.com.

It's since been replaced, but I think the original recipe (while also using more easily accessible ingredients) still holds up really well today!


Time to make:

Prep time: 15 min

Bake time: 55 min

Total Time: 1 hour 10 min

What you'll need:

  • 1 3/4 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 lemon zest
  • 1/2 cup room temp. unsalted butter
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 whole eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1 cup sifted icing sugar
  • 2 to 3 tbsp lemon juice
